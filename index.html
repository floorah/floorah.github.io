Cucumber Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 whole cucumber
  • 1tbsp cream cheese
  • 100g of diced smoked salmon
  • 1/2 a diced red onion
  • 1tsp of capers
  • A chopped handful of dill
  • A healthy pinch of MSG
  • Everything bagel seasoning (or sesame seeds, salt, and black pepper alternatively)

The Viral Salmon Bagel Cucumber Salad

Step 1: Thinly slice the entire cucumber into a large container (sealable is best)

Step 2: Add the cream cheese, salmon, capers, dill, red onion and MSG

Step 3: Seal the container and shake well (alternatively stir until mixed)

Step 4: Top with everything bagel seasoning and enjoy!
